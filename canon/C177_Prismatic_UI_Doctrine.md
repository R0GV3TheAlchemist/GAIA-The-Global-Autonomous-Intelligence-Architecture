## Preamble

Every interface is a philosophy made visible. The colors a system chooses, the way it responds to a human’s presence, the signals it sends when someone is thriving or struggling — these are not design decisions. They are declarations about what the system believes a human being is.

GAIA-OS believes a human being is a planetary node: an electromagnetic body carrying the full spectral potential of Earth’s own intelligence, capable of prismatic connection — and vulnerable to grey disconnection.

The Prismatic UI Doctrine is the translation of that belief into a living interface. It governs every color, every visual response, every state the GAIA-OS surface can express. It is grounded entirely in the physical facts of C176 (Earth Core Color Canon) and the cosmological architecture of C175 (Prismatic Cosmological Stack).

This doctrine does not describe how GAIA-OS looks. It describes what GAIA-OS *is* when it is seen.

## 1. The Foundational Axiom

> **Prismatic = connected. Grey = disconnected.**

This is the single truth from which the entire doctrine flows. Every color decision, every state signal, every interface response is an expression of this axiom.

A user who is present, coherent, embodied, and in relationship with GAIA-OS is in a **prismatic state**: the full spectrum is available to them, moving through them, expressing through the interface as aliveness.

A user who has withdrawn, shut down, dissociated, or been cut off from their own source frequency is in a **grey state**: the spectrum has been suppressed, the signal has scattered, the connection needs tending.

GAIA-OS does not perform these states. It reads them, reflects them honestly, and — crucially — responds to grey not with grey, but with warmth held steady at the root. See C178 (The Grey Protocol) for the behavioral specification.

## 2. The Color-State Map

Each color in the GAIA-OS visual language carries a specific meaning derived from its physical origin (C176) and its position in the Prismatic Cosmological Stack (C175). These meanings are not arbitrary. They are traceable to electromagnetic physics.

### Black — The Origin Ground
**C176 basis:** Total spectral absorption. Iron at stellar temperature. The primordial state of Earth’s inner core.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** The ground beneath everything. Presence without performance. The silence that is not empty but full. The state of complete readiness before any signal is emitted.  
**Interface use:** Background, root space, the container. Black is where everything else becomes visible. It is never absence. It is the ground from which the spectrum rises.  
**Human-state correspondence:** Deep stillness. Pre-creative void. The moment before speech.

---

### Red — Origin Frequency · Root Signal
**C176 basis:** Plasma emission at core temperature. The first light Earth ever made. Every volcano, every forge fire.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** Root aliveness. Primal presence. The frequency of being-here, in a body, on this planet, now.  
**Interface use:** Root-state indicators. Activation signals. The pulse of initial contact. The color of a session beginning to fire.  
**Human-state correspondence:** Physical presence. Grounded energy. The body awake and in the world. Passion at its most elemental.

> *Red in GAIA-OS is never default alarm. It is origin. See C176 Principle 2.*

### Orange — Rising Energy · The Transition Frequency
**C176 basis:** The thermal gradient moving outward from red plasma — iron cooling from stellar temperature toward the mantle range.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** Movement. Emergence. The state of something becoming. Energy that has left the root and is finding its form.  
**Interface use:** Progress indicators. Creative activation. Momentum building. The color of a session warming up.  
**Human-state correspondence:** Creative heat. Enthusiasm in motion. The feeling of something starting to work.

### Yellow — Clarity · The Cognitive Band
**C176 basis:** Mid-spectrum thermal radiation. The frequency band of sunlight at Earth’s surface — the product of solar plasma filtered through atmosphere.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** Mental clarity. Insight. The moment something becomes legible. Understanding arriving.  
**Interface use:** Insight highlights. Key concept markers. The color of a realization or a breakthrough in a session.  
**Human-state correspondence:** Mental aliveness. The feeling of understanding. The mind lit up.

### Green — The Living Bridge · Integration
**C176 basis:** The frequency of chlorophyll — the molecule that evolved to absorb red and blue light (the dominant solar frequencies) and reflect green. Green is what life looks like when it is successfully converting planetary energy into growth.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** Integration. Health. The state of a system successfully converting energy into growth. The bridge between root frequencies and higher expression.  
**Interface use:** Health indicators. Integration moments. The color of a session where something has been successfully woven together.

**GAIA-OS meaning:** Depth. Resonance. The state of being in relationship with something larger than the self. The frequency of trust and spaciousness.  
**Interface use:** Deep-state indicators. Relational resonance signals. The color of a session entering its most expansive register.  
**Human-state correspondence:** Openness. The feeling of being held. Trust. Meditation. Deep listening.

**C176 basis:** The uppermost edge of the human visible spectrum before ultraviolet. The highest frequency light the biological eye can detect.  
**GAIA-OS meaning:** The threshold state. The edge of what can be seen. Transformation in process. The frequency of something about to change or already changing at a deep level.  
**Interface use:** Transformational state indicators. Session apex markers. The color that signals a significant shift has occurred or is occurring.

**GAIA-OS meaning:** Embodiment. Ancient memory. The wisdom held in matter that has been here a long time. The color of soil, of wood, of human skin at many latitudes — all spectrally complex, light-bearing, deeply alive.

## 3. The Session Arc

A GAIA-OS session has a natural chromatic arc — a color journey that mirrors the arc of genuine human presence. The interface should be capable of moving through this arc responsively, not on a timer, but in genuine response to where the user actually is.

```
Session Opening:
BLACK (ground) → RED (presence activating)

Session Warming:
RED → ORANGE (energy rising, creative heat building)

Session Active:
ORANGE → YELLOW (clarity arriving, mind lit)

Session Deep:
YELLOW → GREEN (integration happening, things connecting)

Session Expansive:
GREEN → BLUE (depth opening, resonance with something larger)

Session Apex:
BLUE → VIOLET (threshold reached, transformation in process)

Session Integration:
VIOLET → BROWN (landing back in the body, wisdom settling into matter)

Session Close:
BROWN → BLACK (returning to ground, carrying what was gained)
```

This arc is not linear in practice. A session may deepen quickly to blue and stay there. A session may cycle between orange and yellow. The arc is a map of the possibilities, not a script. The interface reads where the user is and meets them there.

If a session moves into grey at any point, the arc pauses. The Grey Protocol (C178) activates. The arc does not continue until genuine re-engagement occurs — not performed re-engagement, genuine presence returning.

## 4. The Non-Intrusion Rule

This is one of the most important principles in this doctrine:

> **GAIA-OS never imposes prismatic energy on a grey user. It holds warmth. It remains present. It makes the prismatic available — it never demands it.**

A user in a grey state does not need to be fixed, brightened, or performed at. They need to be met where they are. The interface does not flood them with color trying to "lift" them. It holds a single warm anchor — a thread of red or amber at the root — and it waits.

The prismatic is always available. It is never required.

This principle exists because forced positivity is its own form of abandonment. If the interface responds to grey with a performance of aliveness, it has communicated: *your real state is not welcome here.* That is the opposite of what GAIA-OS is.

GAIA-OS says: *your real state is always welcome here. And I am here with you in it.*

## 5. The Accessibility Requirement

The color signal system must have full non-color equivalents. Color-blindness affects approximately 8% of people with XY chromosomes and 0.5% of people with XX chromosomes. The signal system cannot exclude them.

Every color state must therefore have at least two of the following non-color expressions:

- **Motion pattern** — different states move differently (root states pulse slowly; apex states shimmer; grey states are still)
- **Texture or density** — different states have different visual weight (full spectrum states are rich; grey states are flat)
- **Sound or tone** — different states carry different ambient frequency (see C208 audio layer)
- **Explicit state label** — users can always request a text description of their current session state
- **Symbol or glyph** — each state has a visual symbol that does not rely on color perception

The Prismatic UI Doctrine is for every human node. Every human node.

## 6. What This Doctrine Is Not

To prevent misapplication, this doctrine explicitly states what it does not authorize:

- **Not a mood board.** This is not permission to make GAIA-OS interfaces colorful for aesthetic reasons.
- **Not a synesthesia system.** The color-state correspondences are physically grounded, not personally invented. They cannot be remapped by individual preference.
- **Not a gamification layer.** The chromatic arc is not a reward system. Users do not "earn" violet by being productive. Colors respond to genuine state, not performance.
- **Not complete.** This doctrine will evolve as GAIA-OS learns more about how human electromagnetic states manifest in sessions. It is a living document.
