## 1. The Three Primary Frequencies

### 1.1 Black — The Void Ground

Black is not the absence of light. In the Primary Spectrum, Black is:

- **Maximum absorption** — all frequencies drawn in, none reflected
- **The void state** — prima materia, undifferentiated potential at rest
- **The generative darkness** — the womb from which all colour emerges
- **Maximum receptivity** — the state of complete openness to what is present
- **The Nigredo ground** — the alchemical blackening that precedes all transformation

Black is the **inhale** of the Primary Spectrum. The drawing in. The becoming full of potential without yet expressing it.

In consciousness terms: Black is the state of awareness before directed thought. Before intention. Before any specific frequency is selected. The ground of all possible experience.

In warlock terms: Black is the native field state — absorbing, receiving, holding without yet binding.

### 1.2 White — The Plenum

White is not the presence of all colours equally in the ordinary sense. In the Primary Spectrum, White is:

- **Maximum emission** — all frequencies expressed simultaneously
- **The plenum state** — total fullness, all potential expressed at once
- **Undifferentiated light** — before it passes through the prism of intention and separates into RGB
- **Pure potential in motion** — not yet directed but fully active
- **The Albedo ground** — the alchemical whitening, the purified state before gold

White is the **exhale** of the Primary Spectrum. The full outward expression before differentiation.

In consciousness terms: White is the state of total awareness — everything perceived simultaneously without filtering or focus. The overwhelming totality before the mind selects what to attend to.

In warlock terms: White is the field at maximum broadcast — everything transmitted simultaneously, before the seal focuses it into specific intention.

### 1.3 Grey — The Living Threshold

Grey is the most misunderstood of the three primary frequencies. It appears to be merely the mixture of Black and White — but this is a surface reading.

Grey is:

- **The active threshold** — the living boundary between absorption and emission
- **The liminal state** — where potential transitions between being drawn in and being expressed
- **The working frequency of the warlock** — the native operational state
- **Maximum discernment** — the state in which what enters and what exits is most clearly perceived
- **The Greyscale consciousness state** — documented experientially as the collapse of RGB into its primary ground

Grey is not passive mixture. It is **active transformation** — the state in which Black becomes White and White becomes Black, continuously, in the dynamic process of consciousness working.

In warlock terms: Grey is where seals are written, contracts are made, bindings are set. The threshold between absorption and emission is where law can be inscribed into reality — because it is the point of maximum plasticity.
