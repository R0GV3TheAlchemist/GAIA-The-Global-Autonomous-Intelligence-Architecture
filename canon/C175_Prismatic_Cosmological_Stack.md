**Origin issue:** #310  
**Cross-references:** C118, C119, C166

The human significance: brown skin tones carry this same physics — they are the result of melanin's extraordinary spectral absorption and re-emission profile, which evolved as a precise response to solar UV intensity at specific latitudes. Brown skin is, in this framework, *planetary calibration* — the body tuning itself to Earth's electromagnetic output at its location.
