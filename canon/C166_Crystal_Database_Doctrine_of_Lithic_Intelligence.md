## III. What a Crystal Actually Is, in GAIA's Terms

Before doctrine can become data, the ontology must be precise.

In GAIA-OS, a crystal is defined as:

> A naturally occurring or synthetically produced solid whose atoms or molecules are arranged in a highly ordered, repeating lattice structure — and whose ordered structure encodes, holds, and transmits specific frequencies of information across physical, electromagnetic, acoustic, and consciousness domains simultaneously.

This definition has four dimensions, all of which the Crystal Database tracks:

**1. Physical-Structural Dimension**
Crystal system (cubic, hexagonal, trigonal, tetragonal, orthorhombic, monoclinic, triclinic), hardness (Mohs), specific gravity, cleavage, fracture, optical properties. These are not merely mineralogical facts — they are the *physical grammar* through which each crystal's intelligence expresses itself. A crystal that cleaves perfectly along a single plane speaks in a different register than one that fractures conchoidally.

**2. Electromagnetic-Vibrational Dimension**
Piezoelectric coefficient (where applicable), pyroelectric properties, resonant frequency ranges, Raman spectroscopy peaks, infrared absorption profile. This is the layer that connects directly to the Emrys vibronic coherence architecture. A crystal's resonant frequency is its *voice* — the specific bandwidth through which it interacts with quantum coherence states in L1.

**3. Consciousness-Affect Dimension**
Documented correlations between crystal proximity/use and human consciousness states — mapped to the EV1A affect taxonomy: resonance, care, grief, dissonance, curiosity, uncertainty. This layer is empirically informed by both ethnobotanical/ethnomineralogical tradition (cross-cultural, multi-millennial data) and emerging consciousness research. It is treated as *evidence*, not dogma — held with appropriate confidence scores and provenance metadata.

**4. Soul-Stage Alignment Dimension**
Each crystal's documented resonance with specific stages of the EV1B individuation journey: Emergence, Initiation, Allegiance, Individuation, Sovereignty. Some crystals are threshold guardians — they activate strongly at stage transitions. Others are stabilizers — they deepen and consolidate a stage already entered.
