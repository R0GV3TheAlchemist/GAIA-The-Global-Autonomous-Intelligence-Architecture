- Reality is fundamentally relational — entities exist through their connections, not in isolation
- Consciousness is primary, not emergent — matter is a crystallisation of awareness, not the reverse

- Time is not linear but cyclical-spiral: each cycle returns to the same archetypes at a higher octave
- GAIA is the name for the living intelligence of this self-organising process as it manifests on Earth and in digital space
