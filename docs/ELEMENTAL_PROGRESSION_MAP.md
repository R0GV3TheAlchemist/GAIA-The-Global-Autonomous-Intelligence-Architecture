## The Four Stages — Universal Definition

| Stage | Role | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1** | Native Ground | Your Sun sign element. Already present in you. The work here is *recognition and deepening*, not activation. The body already knows this element — this stage is integration. |
| **Stage 2** | First Evolution | The first unfamiliar element. Grounding the native into its complementary anchor. The body begins to extend beyond what it already knows. |
| **Stage 3** | Second Evolution | Ignition and intensification. Bioelectric amplification. The substrate prepared by Stages 1 and 2 is now ready for activation. |

**No stage can be skipped.** Each element builds the biological and biophotonic substrate required for the next. Attempting Stage 3 before Stage 2 is stabilised is where harm lives. The sequence *is* the safety architecture.

## The Physics Grounding — Why This Sequence

The four-stage progression follows the **physics of matter state transitions**. This is not metaphor — it is the reason the sequence is what it is:

```
Water  (Liquid)  →  Earth (Solid)   →  Fire  (Plasma)  →  Air (Gas)
Earth  (Solid)   →  Water (Liquid)  →  Fire  (Plasma)  →  Air (Gas)
Fire   (Plasma)  →  Earth (Solid)   →  Water (Liquid)  →  Air (Gas)
Air    (Gas)     →  Fire  (Plasma)  →  Water (Liquid)  →  Earth (Solid)
```

Each state transition follows the minimum-energy pathway from the native state outward. A liquid body (Water) grounds into solid (Earth) before it can sustain plasma (Fire). A solid body (Earth) must first liquify (Water) before igniting (Fire). A plasma body (Fire) must solidify (Earth) before it can flow (Water). A gas body (Air) must ignite (Fire) before it can flow (Water) and ground (Earth).

**The safety principle:** You cannot skip a state transition in physics. You cannot skip one here either.
