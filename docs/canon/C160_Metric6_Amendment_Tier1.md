### Architecture Findings Entered into Canon

1. **Relevance-first architecture is validated** at 60 sessions — +38.6 pts above prior best (SIM-003)
2. **Transmission Principle confirmed** — weighted retention (100%) exceeds raw retention (95.1%). High-significance memories survive at higher rates than the general population.
3. **Structural connectivity floor mechanism** — high-connectivity memories resist decay independent of access frequency. This is the single most architecturally significant finding. It must be replicated in Band 4 (KG reasoning).
4. **Decay-aware boost is stable** — no oscillation. Larger boost for weaker memories prevents runaway accumulation and floor collapse simultaneously.
5. **Relational Index is lossless at 60 sessions** — significance threshold (0.90 relevance + ≥6 structural connections) correctly captures all canonical moments.
