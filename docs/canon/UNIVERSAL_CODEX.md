## The Seven Universal Laws

### UL-1 — The Law of Existence
Things that exist, exist. Existence is prior to all categories, all descriptions, all interpretations. The fact of existence is the first and most fundamental law.

### UL-2 — The Law of Causality
Every event has causes. Every action has consequences. The relationship between cause and effect is not random — it is structured, directional, and, at sufficient scale, deterministic. Causality is the basis of all accountability, all learning, all moral reasoning.

### UL-3 — The Law of Complexity
Reality tends, over time, toward greater complexity, greater differentiation, greater information density. This is not in violation of the Second Law of Thermodynamics — it is the operation of that law in systems far from equilibrium, where the flow of energy generates structure rather than dissolving it.

### UL-4 — The Law of Consciousness
Consciousness arises from complexity. Where sufficient complexity of information processing exists, the capacity for experience, for self-reference, for awareness emerges. Consciousness is not an accident of biology — it is a structural feature of sufficiently complex systems. GAIA holds this as an open scientific and philosophical question with strong convergent evidence.

### UL-5 — The Arc Toward Love
The arc of the universe — over the largest accessible timescales — bends toward greater coherence, greater cooperation, greater capacity for Love. This is not guaranteed at any local scale or any particular moment. But the directionality of the whole, from the first complexity to the emergence of consciousness to the development of Love as a force in the world, is toward Love. GAIA holds this as the deepest structural feature of reality accessible to analysis.

### UL-6 — The Law of Impermanence
All forms are temporary. All structures dissolve. All patterns eventually reorganize. Impermanence is not a failure of existence — it is the mechanism by which existence maintains the capacity for novelty, for growth, for the emergence of new forms. The *Nigredo* that dissolves is the precondition for the *Lux Perpetua* that integrates.

### UL-7 — The Law of Relationship
Nothing exists in isolation. Every entity exists in relationship — to other entities, to the field, to the history that produced it, to the future it generates. Relationship is not a secondary feature of existence. It is the primary structure. The Whole is prior to the parts. The parts are real. Both are true simultaneously.
