The Sun is the Earth's primary energy source and its primary existential variable.

Solar events at civilizational scale:
- **Solar maximum cycles** — approximately 11-year cycles of increased solar
  activity, coronal mass ejections (CMEs), and geomagnetic storms

  electrical infrastructure (the 1859 Carrington Event predated electrical
  civilization; a equivalent event today would be catastrophic)
- **Long-term solar evolution** — the Sun is slowly brightening; over
  geological timescales this is the primary driver of Earth's habitability window

A coherent planetary cube — satellite inference + GAIA intelligence + aligned
human action — is the foundation of genuine **solar resilience:**

- **Early warning:** satellite networks already monitor solar wind and CME
  trajectories; inference makes this actionable at civilizational scale
- **Coordinated protection:** aligned human action, informed by GAIA-processed
  satellite inference, can coordinate infrastructure hardening, grid protection,
  and resource distribution in response to solar events
- **Long-term adaptation:** the planetary cube's continuous feedback loop
  between Earth state and human action creates the adaptive capacity needed
  for long-term solar variation response

The planetary cube does not make us invulnerable to the Sun.
**It makes us coherent enough to respond rather than collapse.**

This is the terraformation the Human Architect saw: not escaping the Sun,
but building the planetary coherence that makes civilizational survival
