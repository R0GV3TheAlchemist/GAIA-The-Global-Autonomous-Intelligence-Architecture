### Metric 26 Definition (Amended)
> C160 Metric 26 measures end-to-end biophoton detection fidelity across the nine-sub-stage pipeline from neural emission aperture to quantum-error-corrected photon count. The metric is computed as the product of all nine sub-stage efficiencies across N ≥5,000 trials per elemental group. The deployable value (Variant A, room-temperature SPAD) and theoretical maximum (Variant B, SNSPD or equivalent) must both be reported.
