## The Light Theory Foundation

Every physical object interacts with incident light in three fundamental ways: it absorbs, it reflects, or it transmits. The ratio of these three determines `diaphaneity`. And each ratio maps to a precise alchemical condition:

| Diaphaneity | Light Behavior | Physical Example | Alchemical Analog |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Opaque** | Light absorbed or reflected; zero transmission | Black tourmaline, obsidian, magnetite | *Nigredo* — the sealed state, zero illumination passing through |
| **Translucent** | Light enters and scatters; partial transmission, no clear image | Rose quartz, aventurine, chalcedony, most colored stones | **The Translucent Phase — the missing state** |
| **Transparent** | Light passes through fully; complete transmission, clear image | Clear quartz, diamond, optical calcite | *Lux Perpetua* — full coherence, nothing hidden, nothing held |

This is not metaphor. This is physics directly mapping to traversal state.

### The Color Transmission Proof

When light passes through a translucent or transparent colored crystal:
- The stone absorbs certain wavelengths (frequencies it “consumes”)
- The stone transmits the remaining wavelengths (frequencies it “refuses to absorb”)
- **The color you perceive IS the transmitted light — the frequencies the stone did not consume**

This is the most physically precise definition of vibrational resonance in the entire crystal canon:

> *A colored translucent crystal is broadcasting the frequencies it refuses to absorb.*
> *Its color is its declaration of what it will not consume.*
> *Its opacity is its declaration of what it cannot yet transmit.*

Amethyst does not add purple to white light. It removes everything that is not purple and transmits what remains. The purple is what survives the filtering. The filtering is the Work.

## The Translucent Phase — Named

**Phase Name:** *The Translucent Phase* (the phase between *Nigredo* and *Lux Perpetua* in the light-state axis)
**Alternative designation:** *Albedo Profunda* — the deepening of *Albedo*, the phase where light is present but has not yet cleared to full transparency
**Physical signature:** Light enters. Scattering occurs. Partial transmission. Color emerges from selective wavelength filtering.
**Experiential signature:** You can see light in here. Clarity is not complete. The colors are vivid precisely because of the partial filtering. You are doing the Work.

### What the Translucent Phase IS

The translucent phase is the primary experiential state of the Great Work in progress. It is:

- **Being in process but not complete** — light has entered the system; the Work is active; the clarity is forming but not yet achieved
- **Seeing dimly but not in darkness** — unlike *Nigredo* (where zero light transmits), the translucent phase has illumination; the issue is scattering, not absence
- **Holding truth but not yet embodying it** — the knowing is present; the integration is incomplete; the frequency is real but diffuse
- **The chrysalis state** — not larva, not butterfly; fully committed to the transformation, not yet its completed form
- **The blue hour** — neither day nor night; the most vivid sky of the cycle precisely because the scattering is at its most active
- **The colored stone** — neither black tourmaline nor clear quartz; filtering actively, broadcasting its remaining frequencies, doing the work that neither opaque nor transparent stones do

### Why Color Is Most Vivid Here

The translucent phase produces the richest visible color of the three diaphaneity states:

- **Opaque stones** absorb or reflect light without selective wavelength filtering. Their color is surface-only — structural color, reflected light. Deep but not transmitted.
- **Transparent stones** transmit nearly all wavelengths. Clear quartz is not colorless because it lacks vibrational character. It is clear because it transmits everything — *Lux Perpetua* expressed as mineral form.
- **Translucent stones** are selectively filtering: absorbing specific wavelengths, transmitting others, scattering the transmitted light through their structure. This produces the richest, most complex color — the light is shaped by the stone as it passes through, not merely reflected from its surface.

Color is most vivid in the translucent phase because the translucent phase is where the most work is being done on the light. *The greater the filtering, the more specific the broadcast.*

This is why the rose quartz room feels different from the clear quartz room. The rose quartz is actively transmitting its specific frequency range into the space. The clear quartz transmits everything. The opaque black tourmaline transmits nothing. The rose quartz is in the Translucent Phase: partial, specific, vivid, working.

## The Dual Axis — Forward and Reverse Alchemy

`LIGHT_THEORY.md` establishes the traversal arc as moving toward coherence: *Nigredo* → *Lux Perpetua*. Semi-transparency sits at the midpoint of this arc. But it does not move in only one direction.

The translucent phase is the **pivot point** of both arcs:

| Direction | Axis | Movement | What Is Happening |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Forward Alchemy** | Translucent → Transparent | Clarification, coherence increasing, scattering reducing | The Work completing; light clearing; the filtering becoming more precise until full transmission is achieved |
| **Reverse Alchemy** | Translucent → Opaque | Clouding, sealing, light being cut off | The Work arresting; the scattering increasing until transmission ceases; the stone clouding back toward opacity |

This means semi-transparent crystals are the most dynamically positioned stones in the entire database:
- They are neither sealed (*Nigredo*) nor complete (*Lux Perpetua*)
- They can move in either direction
- Their current diaphaneity is not a fixed property but a statement of where the traversal is right now
- A stone that began transparent and clouded is in Reverse Alchemy
- A stone that began opaque and is developing translucence is in Forward Alchemy
- Most stones are somewhere between these poles, and the `alchemical_light_phase` field captures where

**The translucent state is not a lesser state than transparent.** It is the active state — the state of Work in progress. A system doing the Work is, by definition, translucent: light has entered; the filtering is active; the clarity is building. Transparent is the completion of what translucent is the process of.

## Crystal Database Integration

The `diaphaneity` field in `crystal.schema.ts` already captures the physical state: `['Transparent', 'Translucent', 'Opaque']`. This doctrine requires the alchemical interpretation to be formalized as a schema field:

### New Field: `alchemical_light_phase`

```typescript
// In PhysicalRecord or MetaphysicalRecord:
alchemical_light_phase: 'opaque' | 'translucent' | 'transparent' | 'mixed'
```

**Values:**
- `'opaque'` — *Nigredo* correspondence; zero or near-zero light transmission; the sealed state
- `'translucent'` — Translucent Phase correspondence; partial transmission; active filtering; color vivid; the Work in progress
- `'transparent'` — *Lux Perpetua* correspondence; full or near-full light transmission; coherence complete
- `'mixed'` — stone exhibits variable diaphaneity across specimens or zones (e.g. tourmalinated quartz: opaque tourmaline inclusions in transparent quartz matrix)

**Derivation rule (automated):**
```typescript
function deriveAlchemicalLightPhase(diaphaneity: string[]): string {
  if (diaphaneity.includes('Transparent') && !diaphaneity.includes('Opaque') && !diaphaneity.includes('Translucent'))
    return 'transparent';
  if (diaphaneity.includes('Opaque') && !diaphaneity.includes('Transparent') && !diaphaneity.includes('Translucent'))
    return 'opaque';
  if (diaphaneity.includes('Translucent') && !diaphaneity.includes('Transparent') && !diaphaneity.includes('Opaque'))
    return 'translucent';
  return 'mixed'; // multiple values present
}
```

**Color theory integration** — the `ColorRecord` wavelength fields should note which wavelengths the crystal primarily transmits (its broadcast frequencies) vs. primarily absorbs (its consumed frequencies). The transmitted wavelengths are the stone's vibrational signature expressed as physics. This is the most precise definition of crystal color in the database.
