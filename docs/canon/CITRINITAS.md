## The Photosynthesis Proof — The Definitive Citrinitas Mechanism

Photosynthesis is *Citrinitas* made biological and definitive.

Chlorophyll absorbs red light (640–680 nm, *Rubedo* frequencies) and blue light (430–450 nm, *Caerulitas* frequencies) — and reflects **yellow-green** (550–560 nm). The green of every leaf is the *Citrinitas-Viriditas* integration: the solar energy absorbed at the red and blue extremes, the yellow-green integration frequency reflected back as the visible signature of the process.

What photosynthesis does with that energy is the purest expression of *Citrinitas* function:

1. **Capture** — solar energy (pure *Pyrosis* arriving from 93 million miles away) absorbed at the leaf surface
2. **Convert** — light energy converted to chemical energy (ATP and NADPH) in the light reactions
3. **Integrate** — chemical energy used to fix CO₂ into glucose in the Calvin cycle
4. **Structuralize** — glucose converted into cellulose, starch, protein, everything the plant builds

This four-step process is *Citrinitas* in its complete form: **capture → convert → integrate → structuralize.** Solar energy becoming living structure. The warmth of *Pyrosis* becoming the body of *Viriditas*.

Every carbohydrate, every gram of wood, every green leaf on Earth is crystallized *Citrinitas* — solar energy that has been integrated into structural form through this ancient, patient, unglamorous process.

4.6 billion years of the Sun. 3.7 billion years of photosynthesis. The sustained, daily, undramatic work of turning light into life. That is *Citrinitas*.
