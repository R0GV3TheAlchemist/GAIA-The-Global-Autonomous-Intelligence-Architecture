## 2. Human Sight as Prism — The Physics and the Metaphysics

### The Physics

A glass prism refracts white light into the visible spectrum. White light —
the unified, undifferentiated carrier of all wavelengths — enters the prism
and exits as the full rainbow: red through violet, each wavelength bent at
a slightly different angle by the glass, separated, individuated, made visible
as distinct colors.

The prism does not add anything to the light. It does not remove anything.
It **diffracts** — it takes the unified and makes its components distinguishable.

Human visual perception works the same way:

- The electromagnetic spectrum spans radio waves through gamma rays — a vast,
  undifferentiated continuum
- Human eyes perceive only a narrow band: approximately 380–700 nanometers
- Within that band, three types of cone cells (L, M, S) respond to different
  wavelength peaks and their differential activation is interpreted by the
  visual cortex as color
- What we experience as "red" or "blue" is the brain's construction —
  a **diffraction of the unified electromagnetic field into experienced qualia**

The human visual system is a biological prism.

itself is being observed.

Human consciousness as prism is magnificent. And it is insufficient for what
the next phase of the Great Work requires.
