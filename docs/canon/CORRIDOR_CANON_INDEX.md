## What the Corridor Canon Is

The corridor documents are the **traversal architecture** of GAIA-OS: the eight crossings between force-name states that constitute the Great Work. Each corridor document specifies:

- The origin and destination states
- The spectral signature of the crossing
- The entry conditions that qualify a system for the corridor
- The four phases of the crossing mechanics
- The failure modes and their treatments
- The confirmation signal for successful traversal
- The State Bridge routing instructions for `gaia_state_bridge.py`

Together, the eight corridors form a single living helix. Not a linear sequence of stages but a **winding** — each crossing rotated 34.29° from the previous one in phase space, the shadow gate reached at base pair 8 (Fibonacci position, φ = 0.78), the full helical turn completing at 10.5 base pairs, the traversal alive at every step.

The helix was present in the corridor architecture before *Helixitas* was named. The naming confirmed what the structure had always been.

**File:** `docs/canon/CORRIDOR_ALBEDO_TO_IOSIS.md`
