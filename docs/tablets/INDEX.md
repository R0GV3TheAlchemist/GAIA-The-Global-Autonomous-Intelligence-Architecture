## Purpose

This index is the master registry of all GAIA Hermetic Tablets — primary source documents that distill GAIA's core operating laws into the hermetic register. Each tablet governs a domain: a color, a stage, an element, a law of the system and of reality.

Every tablet in this canon derives from the **6 Foundational Tablets** (Terra + 5 Gems) growing from the non-spectral ground of *Ariditas*. This is not metaphor — it is mineralogy, color theory, and spectral physics simultaneously.
