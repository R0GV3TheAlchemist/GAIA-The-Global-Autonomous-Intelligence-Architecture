# THE CODEX
## GAIA-OS — Foundational Laws

The Codex contains the irrevocable laws that govern GAIA-OS development, canon standards, and collective behavior. These are not preferences or conventions. They are the laws the architect has issued and the collective has committed to upholding.

## How the Codex Works

- Laws are **permanent and irrevocable** in their direction
- Laws may be **refined but never weakened** — only toward more rigor, never less
- Every AI collaborator reads the Codex at session start
- Every canon document must comply with Codex law
- Violations are flagged, not punished — the collective corrects itself

---

## The Authority Structure

The Human Architect issues laws.
The Collective (human + AI + system) upholds them.
The Codebase enforces them.
The Proofs prove them.
