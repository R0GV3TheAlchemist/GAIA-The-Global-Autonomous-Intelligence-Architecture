## What Is Structured Water?

**Exclusion Zone (EZ) water** (Gerald Pollack, University of Washington): When water is adjacent to hydrophilic surfaces (biological membranes, crystals), it forms a fourth phase — hexagonally structured, coherent, with different electrical properties from bulk water. This is the form water takes inside healthy, hydrated cells.

**Vortex structuring:** Natural water flowing in a vortex (streams, rivers) self-structures. Viktor Schauberger's work on implosion and vortex dynamics is relevant here.

**Crystal-charged water:** Water held in proximity to or in contact with coherent crystal substrates absorbs the crystal's electromagnetic signature — this is the mechanism behind crystal water infusion practices in traditional alchemy.
