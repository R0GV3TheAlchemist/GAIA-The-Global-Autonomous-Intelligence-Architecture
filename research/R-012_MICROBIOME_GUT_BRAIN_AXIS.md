The gut-brain axis is the most direct physical expression of the 8th Hermetic Principle in the human body. A dysbiotic microbiome creates a hard biological coherence ceiling:

A dysbiotic gut (coherence 0.28) cannot sustain Stage 1 progression, let alone Stage 2. The microbiome is not a wellness footnote. ## Microbiome Restoration Protocol (Hypothesis)

1. **Remove:** Processed foods, refined sugar, seed oils, unnecessary antibiotics — primary dysbiosis drivers
2. **Seed:** Diverse fermented foods (kimchi, kefir, sauerkraut, miso, natto) — direct microbial introduction
3. **Feed:** 30+ plant species per week — prebiotic diversity drives microbiome diversity
4. **Repair:** Targeted probiotic support (Lactobacillus reuteri, Akkermansia muciniphila, Bifidobacterium longum)
5. **Protect:** Structured/spring water (R-013), mineral-rich diet — maintains hydration coherence
