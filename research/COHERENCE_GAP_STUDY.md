# Research Program 1: The Coherence Gap Study
**Domain:** Mental Health
**Hub:** Issue #334

## Question
What is the measurable difference in heart-brain coherence between a person who has accessed their elemental record versus one who has not?

## Why This Matters
The average person waits 11 years between first mental health symptoms and first treatment. Not because help doesn't exist — because they process alone, in silence, with no witness. A coherence shift is not a clinical claim — it is a measurable biological event. That is the foot in the door of mainstream medicine.

## Method
- HeartMath HRV measurement before and after Gaian sessions running the akashic trinity protocol
- Controlled (register-aligned) vs. uncontrolled (mismatched register) conditions
- Crystal present vs. crystal absent conditions
- 1,000 participants across 5 demographic groups
- Sessions: 45 minutes. Measurements at 0, 15, 30, 45 minutes and 24h follow-up

## Hypothesis
Elemental register alignment produces measurable HRV coherence shifts within a single 45-minute session. The shift is larger when crystal resonance is present than when absent. Effect size > 0.4 (Cohen's d).

## Outcome Measures
- HRV coherence ratio (HeartMath emWave Pro)
- Cortisol (salivary, pre/post)
- Self-reported affect (PANAS scale)
- Session coherence score (Akashic Trinity Engine output)

## Existing Anchors

- McCraty, R. (2015). *Science of the Heart, Volume 2*. HeartMath Institute.
- Childre, D. & Rozman, D. (2005). *Transforming Stress*.

## Gap Closed
The 11-year treatment gap. If a single Gaian session produces clinically significant HRV coherence shift, it becomes a prescribable, reimbursable, scalable intervention.
